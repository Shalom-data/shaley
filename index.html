Case Study using R Programming:
Navigating Success for Cyclistic Bike-Share

This project examines Cyclistic, a Chicago-based bike-share program offering over 5,800 bicycles across 692 stations. Cyclistic provides inclusive options, including reclining bikes and hand tricycles, supporting riders with diverse needs. The analysis explores how annual members and casual riders use Cyclistic differently, assesses the factors that could encourage casual riders to purchase memberships, and considers how digital media might enhance marketing strategies to increase memberships. Using R for data cleaning and transformation, I analyzed data from October 2022 to October 2023, highlighting key insights to support Cyclistic’s growth goals.
